Frequently asked questions

What is MCP?

MCP (Model Context Protocol) is a standard for connecting AI assistants to external tools and data. It's supported by Claude, and lets AI actually take actions in your systems — not just chat about them.

Do I need to install anything?

For the hosted version, no. Just connect your Intercom account via OAuth and add the MCP endpoint to your AI client (Claude Desktop, etc.). If you self-host, you'll need Node.js.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use OAuth 2.0 and never store your Intercom API keys. Tokens are encrypted at rest and in transit. You can revoke access anytime from your Intercom settings.
